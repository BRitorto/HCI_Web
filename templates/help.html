    Help

    How to add a Room?

    1. Click on the section “Rooms”
    2. Click on “Add Room”
    3. Write the name of the new Room
    4. Click on "Save changes"
    Other way:

    From “Main Page”, in the “Quick Actions” section, click on “Add Room” and follow steps 3 and 4 from the above list.

    How to add a Device?

    1. Click on the section “Rooms”
    2. Select the room in which the device will be placed
    3. Select the type of device you want to add
    4. Click on “add device”. This will open up a new window
    5. Fill in the required fields
    Other way:

    From “Main Page”, in the “Quick Actions” section, click on “Add Device”. You will need to choose the room in which the device will be, and then fill in the required fields.

    How to change a device's settings?

    1. Click on the section “Rooms”
    2. Select the room in which the device is in
    3. Click on the wanted type of device
    4. Click on the trash can to the rightmost corner of the device’s name

    How to delete a room?

    1. Click on the section “Rooms”
    2. Click on the trash can to the rightmost corner of the room’s name

    How to delete a routine?

    1. Click on the section “Routines”
    2. Click on the trash can icon on the right of the wanted routine’s name

    How to use the search bar?

    1. Click on the search bar located at the top right corner of the page
    2. Introduce the concept that you wish to find on the page
    3. Click on any of the options shown in the list that will appear under the search bar
